THE SEVEN WONDERS OF THE WORLD

Here are seven famous places around the world: 1.The Eiffel Tower (Paris, France) An iconic symbol of France, the Eiffel Tower attracts millions of visitors every year. 2.The Great Wall of China (China) One of the most impressive architectural feats in history, stretching over 13,000 miles across northern China. 3.Machu Picchu (Peru) An ancient Inca city set high in the Andes, Machu Picchu is known for its breathtaking views and mysterious ruins. 4.The Colosseum (Rome, Italy) click here A remarkable amphitheater in the heart of Rome, the Colosseum is a symbol of ancient Roman architecture and entertainment. 5.The Taj Mahal (Agra, India) A stunning white marble mausoleum, the Taj Mahal is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, known for its beauty and symmetry. 6.Christ the Redeemer (Rio de Janeiro, Brazil) A towering statue of Jesus Christ that overlooks the city of Rio de Janeiro and is a symbol of Christianity. 7.The Pyramids of Giza (Egypt) Ancient structures that have stood the test of time, the Pyramids of Giza are one of the most enduring symbols of ancient Egypt.
